About Us

Established in 2018, Principle Industries is a leading agri-tech company focused on addressing the needs of Indian farmers.

We provide GSM mobile starters, smart irrigation systems, and sensor-based automation solutions that make farming easier, more affordable, and more productive.

Our flagship brand Siddhant represents trust, innovation, and performance in the field of agricultural automation.
